== Description ==
**Just PDF Invoices and Packing Slips** is a powerful yet lightweight WooCommerce plugin that automatically generates professional PDF invoices and packing slips for your store.

PDF invoices are automatically attached to order confirmation emails and are also available for download from the admin and customer order pages. Say goodbye to manual invoicing and enjoy a streamlined order management process with fully customisable templates and settings.

👉 **Generate beautiful, compliant PDF invoices and packing slips with zero hassle.**

**Key Features:**
✅ **Automatic PDF generation** – Invoices and packing slips created instantly  
✉️ **Attach PDFs to emails** – New order, processing, completed, and more  
🛒 **Download from admin** – Quickly print invoices from the order page  
📂 **Customer access** – Customers can download their invoice from My Account  
⚙️ **Customisable templates** – Adjust layout, branding, labels, and fields  
🌍 **Multilingual and multi-currency support** – Global store ready

== Installation ==

**From the WordPress Admin:**
1. Navigate to **Plugins > Add New**.
2. Search for **"Just PDF Invoices and Packing Slips"**.
3. Click **Install Now**, then **Activate**.

**Manual Installation:**
1. Download the plugin ZIP file from the [WordPress Repository](https://wordpress.org/plugins/just-pdf-invoices-and-packing-slips) or [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/JustThereUK/Just-PDF-Invoices-and-PackingSlips).
2. Upload the extracted folder to **wp-content/plugins/**.
3. Go to **Plugins > Installed Plugins**, find "Just PDF Invoices and Packing Slips", and click **Activate**.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can I customise the PDF templates? =
Yes. You can customise the layout, colors, logo, fields, and text labels to match your branding.

= Which emails include the PDF invoice? =
By default, the invoice is attached to new order, processing order, completed order, and customer invoice emails. You can change this in settings.

= Can customers download invoices from their account? =
Yes! Customers can download their invoices directly from their My Account order history.

= Is this plugin compatible with multilingual and multi-currency plugins? =
Yes, it works with major translation and currency switcher plugins.

= Does this plugin support tax, shipping, and discounts? =
Absolutely. All standard WooCommerce order data is included and displayed clearly.

= Will it slow down my store? =
No. The plugin is lightweight and performance-focused.
